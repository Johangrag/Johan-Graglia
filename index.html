About Me

I am a French physicist currently doing my PhD at the University of Paul Sabatier, at the LNCMI in Toulouse. My research mainly focuses on high-temperature superconductivity — an exciting area of quantum physics.

I’m also interested in cosmology and particle physics, and I enjoy teaching and science outreach.
