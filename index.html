Johan Graglia

PhD student LNCMI - Toulouse

I am a French physicist currently doing my PhD at the University of Paul Sabatier, at the LNCMI in Toulouse. My research mainly focuses on high-temperature superconductivity, an exciting area of quantum physics. While this is my main focus, I’m also interested in other fields of physics like cosmology and particle physics.

I really enjoy teaching and sharing knowledge. On this site, I will share my various projects, mainly related to my thesis and quantum physics, but also those I worked on in cosmology and astrophysics during my studies.
